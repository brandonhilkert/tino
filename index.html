Tino's Last Ride

Martino Araujo passed away peacefully in his sleep on March 16th, 2017 at the age of 50. He leaves behind his beloved wife Suzie of 24 years and 2 children he was tremendously proud of, CJ and Tony.

As an avid motorcyclist, Tino spent hours planning solo and group rides through various parts of the country. For the last few years, he had his sights set on a solo trip across the country spanning 30 days where he'd do nothing but ride, camp, and see the best that nature had to offer. He'd planned everything down to the number of miles he'd ride each day, to the exact location of each campsite.

Sadly, he never got the chance to take this ride, but Suzie, CJ, and Tony want to do it for him. As a family, they want embark on the exact trip he planned (with maybe a few hotels mixed in).

As friends and family, we can help support their desire. By donating to their trip, you'll be able to help them make this 30-day trip across the country seeing the sights that Tino had only dreamed of.
